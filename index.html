Bio

Rasul Hemmati is a dedicated Ph.D. candidate pursuing a degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Oklahoma. His GPA stands at a perfect 4.00 out of 4.00, showcasing his unwavering dedication to academic excellence. Prior to his current pursuit, Rasul earned a Master’s Degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Marquette University in 2021, where he achieved a commendable GPA of 3.8 out of 4.00. His academic journey began at Shiraz University of Technology in Iran, where he completed his first Master’s Degree in Electrical Engineering with a specialization in Power Electronics and Electrical Machines in 2014. Before that, he earned his Bachelor’s Degree in Electrical Engineering with a focus on Power from Urmia University in Iran in 2011.
